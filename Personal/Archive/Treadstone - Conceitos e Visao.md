## Meta Structure (ontologia antiga)

**System Goals:**
- Establish a vision for myself
- Plan the steps to get closer to the vision
- Prioritize and balance the execution
- Make progress measurable
- Be simple and robust (robust to stops/starts, easy to operate and restart)

**Vision Building Elements:** what is my perfect day like; what is my worst day; if no one was around, what would I do to be happy; do-be-have

**Directives:** Principles, values, playbooks, operational rules (e.g., read yourself a Mantra everyday)

**Organizational Elements (old ontology):**
- **Task** — quasi-atomic single day activity
- **Mission** — long tasks requiring research, exploration and planning (with Briefing/Report/Debriefing)
- **Routine** — recurring Tasks with definite intervals or triggers
- **Program** — long endeavor with unclear steps and indefinite duration (Vision elements + Trimester OKRs)
- **Project** — long endeavor with a clear ending
