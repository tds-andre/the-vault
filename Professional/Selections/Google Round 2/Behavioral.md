## Overall Strategy
- Choose N projects (radar, ITM, Prefects, Migration, Captcha)
	- Tag what they showcase
	- challenges, mistakes, technical decisions, choices of technologies (and tradeoffs of these), and the things you would do differently
	-  follow-up questions, like how you would scale the application.
- How to be impressive without being arrogant? Be specific!
	- Just give the facts, don't give the adjectives
- Limit details
	- "I can go into more details if you'd like"
- Response structure
	- Nugget
	- SAR (Situation, Action, Result) - (Situation, Challenges, Addresses) ![](Pasted%20image%2020251117183607.png)
	- Expose personality in your stories
- I WAS GREAT AT DISSECTING AND DISTRIBUTING
