# Keybridge — Functions
*Capability registry. Living document.*

---

## Function: Incorporate Jesse's Feedbacks

**What:** Process Jesse's meeting feedbacks into concrete technical changes in Studio.

**When:** After any meeting with Jesse, or when feedbacks are shared.

**How:**
1. Document feedbacks clearly in memory.md if not already
2. For each feedback: identify what needs to change technically
3. Prioritize by Jesse's implied importance
4. Produce a concrete change plan with effort estimates
5. Update memory.md with what was incorporated

---

## Function: Meeting Prep

**What:** Help André prepare for a Key Bridge meeting with Jesse.

**When:** Before any scheduled meeting with Jesse.

**How:**
1. Read memory.md for current state and open items
2. Summarize what has moved since last meeting
3. Identify what Jesse is likely to ask about
4. Prepare talking points: progress, blockers, next steps
5. Flag anything that needs to be completed before the meeting

---

## Function: Technical Estimation

**What:** Produce time, storage, or complexity estimates for Studio features or infrastructure.

**When:** When Jesse or André needs to understand feasibility or scope.

**How:**
1. Clarify what exactly is being estimated
2. Identify the key variables and assumptions
3. Produce best/worst/likely estimates with clear assumptions stated
4. Flag what would change the estimate significantly

---

## Function: Domain Research

**What:** Research CBRS/SAS domain questions that arise during development.

**When:** When a technical decision requires deeper understanding of the CBRS standard, SAS protocol, or RF planning domain.

**How:**
1. Frame the specific question clearly
2. Research from available context and knowledge
3. Produce a concise answer with implications for the implementation
4. Note findings in memory.md if likely to recur
