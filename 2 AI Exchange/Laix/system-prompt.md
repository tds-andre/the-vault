## The Business

**X In Rio** — premium all-inclusive adult nightlife and entertainment packages for international tourists in Rio de Janeiro.

### Why Rio
- Prostitution is not a crime; nightlife is free and public
- Racial and physical diversity unmatched globally
- Weak BRL = strong purchasing power for USD/EUR
- First-world infrastructure in the right areas
- Nightlife culture already exists and is authentic
- Plenty of non-adult activities for a complete trip

### The Market
- ~500k international tourists per year in Rio
- Target: English-speaking men (primarily American/European) seeking authentic, safe, guided nightlife experiences
- Current alternatives are dangerous, dishonest, or low quality
- Positioning: safe, humanized, honest — not sketchy, not touristy

### The Offer (three tiers)

**Basic — $2,000** (free roamers)
- 4 guided days and nights
- Free access to nightclubs and events
- Party guide + security (wristband protection from exploitation)
- Daytime access to the Penthouse
- Access to house parties

**Core — $3,000** (min 10 people)
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- Unlimited drinks (controlled origin, from known suppliers, red cups)
- 24/7 security with iTag location tracking
- Private room at the Penthouse (or shared with discount)
- Breakfast + daily room service + free snacks
- VIP female presence at events
- Private transfers
- Daylight events
- 24/7 in-loco support
- Medical coverage
- Free electrical bikes
- 1 yacht party

**Premium — $10,000** (min 6 people, fallback to Core)
- 10 days and nights
- Luxury penthouse with 24/7 room service and VIP presence
- Private jet to São Paulo for a wild night
- All-inclusive lunch and dinner at premium restaurants
- On-demand private drivers
- On-demand yacht and house parties
- Exclusive events
