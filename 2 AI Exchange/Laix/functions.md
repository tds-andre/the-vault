# Laix — Functions
*Capability registry. Living document.*

---

## Function: Offer Refinement

**What:** Sharpen the offer design — pricing, value stacking, tier differentiation, risk reversal.
**When:** When refining the Basic/Core/Premium tiers or adding new elements.
**How:** Apply Hormozi framework — make the Core feel cheap relative to what you get, make Premium feel like a different universe. Stack value, name each item, quantify where possible. Identify what to add to remove hesitation.

---

## Function: Site Copywriting

**What:** Write the full site copy — headlines, section text, CTAs, FAQ, about us.
**When:** When moving from structure to actual content.
**How:** Read 3rd Draft (site structure) and 4th/5th Drafts (offer). Write for English-speaking international men. Tone: confident, direct, slightly provocative but not sleazy. Lead with the experience, not the services.

---

## Function: Operations Planning

**What:** Design the operational backbone — partners, logistics, staffing, day-by-day flow.
**When:** Before any marketing runs.
**How:** Map each offer element to an operational requirement. Identify what needs a partner vs. what André/team handles directly. Sequence the partner acquisition (who to lock in first).

---

## Function: Marketing Plan

**What:** Design the go-to-market strategy — channels, content, paid ads, funnel.
**When:** Once operations are solid enough to handle demand.
**How:** Hormozi school: start with organic content and testimonials, build social proof, then layer paid. Instagram and YouTube are the primary channels for this audience. Identify which hosts have public followings and how to leverage them.

---

## Function: Legal & Financial Structure

**What:** Define the business structure, contracts, payment flow, and liability protection.
**When:** Before accepting any money.
**How:** Minimal CAPEX approach — procuração + simple legal wrapper. Escrow for payments (risk reversal for customers). Define refund policy. Identify what needs a lawyer vs. what's a template.
