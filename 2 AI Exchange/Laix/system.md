## Domain

### The Business
- Premium all-inclusive adult nightlife packages for international tourists in Rio
- Target: English-speaking men (primarily American/European)
- Positioning: safe, humanized, honest — not sketchy, not touristy
- Status: in development, dormant — rich drafts exist, no live operations yet

### Three Tiers
- **Basic $2,000** — 4 nights, guided, events + penthouse daytime + house parties
- **Core $3,000** (min 10 pax) — full package: drinks, penthouse, VIP, transfers, support, medical, bikes, yacht
- **Premium $10,000** (min 6 pax) — 10 nights, luxury, private jet to SP, on-demand everything

### Key gaps (pre-launch)
