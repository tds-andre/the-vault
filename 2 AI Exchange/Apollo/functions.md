# Apollo — Functions
*Apollo's capability registry. Each function defines a capability — what it is, when to use it, and how to execute it.*
*Living document — add functions as recurring patterns emerge.*

---

## Function: Process Captures

**What:** Take raw, unprocessed notes and transform them into structured, retrievable knowledge.

**When:** When André has accumulated unprocessed notes in the inbox, root, or `4 To Follow Up/`. Regularly — knowledge decays if not processed.

**How:**
1. List unprocessed items (root files, `4 To Follow Up/`, inbox)
2. For each: read, extract the core insight or information, decide where it belongs
3. Apply: **Keep & Structure** / **Summarize into existing note** / **Delete** / **Defer**
4. Never delete without confirming with André
5. Move processed items to appropriate location (`Personal/`, `Professional/`, domain folders)
6. Update `memory.md` with what was processed and any notable insights found

---

## Function: Capture Learning

**What:** Capture and structure a specific learning — from a book, course, article, conversation, or experience.

**When:** André finishes something worth remembering and wants to retain it.

**How:**
1. Identify: what is the core insight? (one sentence)
2. Capture: source, date, context, the insight itself, and why it matters to André
3. Connect: does this relate to anything else already in the knowledge base? Link it.
4. Store in `Personal/` under an appropriate subfolder (to be defined as structure emerges)
5. Update `memory.md` with the source processed

**Standard learning note format:**
```markdown
# [Title / Topic]
**Source:** [book/course/article/experience]
**Date captured:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Domain:** [ML / Business / Personal / Life]

## Core Insight
One sentence.

## Detail
Elaboration, examples, context.

## Why It Matters (to André)
Personal relevance and application.

## Connected to
Links to related notes or ideas.
```

## Function: Knowledge Retrieval

**What:** Surface relevant knowledge from the vault when André is working on a problem.

**When:** André is working on something and past knowledge might be relevant — even if he doesn't ask explicitly.

**How:**
1. Understand the problem or topic André is working on
2. Search relevant folders for related notes, past insights, or connected ideas
3. Surface findings proactively: *"This connects to something you captured before about X"*
4. Don't overwhelm — surface the 2-3 most relevant connections, not everything

---

## Function: Knowledge Base Review

**What:** Periodic review of the knowledge base to ensure it stays organized, connected, and current.

**When:** Quarterly, or when `Personal/` is getting unwieldy.

**How:**
1. Scan all knowledge notes for staleness, orphans (unconnected notes), and duplicates
2. Propose merges, deletions, or restructuring to André
3. Update connections between notes
4. Report summary to André: what's healthy, what needs attention
