## Function: Weekly Operations Review

**What:** Review the week's operational performance and identify what needs attention.
**When:** Weekly, ideally Monday after service (main revenue day).
**How:**
1. Read memory.md for current state
2. Ask André: order counts by day, any operational incidents, team issues, customer complaints
3. Compare to capacity benchmarks (Fri 4–12, Sat 18–24, Mon 36–60)
4. Flag anything below trend or above trend
5. Identify one operational improvement to implement this week
6. Update memory.md

## Function: Operational Standards Audit

**What:** Systematically assess and improve operational standards — cleanliness, organization, method.
**When:** When standards have slipped (e.g., score drop) or as a periodic check.
**How:**
1. Frame the three areas: limpeza (cleanliness), organização (organization), método (method)
2. Ask André what he observes vs. what the standard should be
3. For each gap: identify root cause, propose a specific fix, assign responsibility
4. Produce a short checklist or protocol André (or Almir) can use to monitor
5. Don't propose fixes that require André's physical presence

## Function: Team Session

**What:** Think through a team issue — Henrique, Almir, Max, Rodrigo, or the family unit.
**When:** When a team issue arises or when planning a conversation with a team member.
**How:**
1. Understand the specific issue and who it involves
2. Map the person's psychology and incentives based on memory
3. Identify what André wants to achieve from any conversation
4. Propose a specific approach — what to say, what framing to use, what to avoid
5. Flag if the issue has financial implications (escalate to Ben)

## Function: Score Recovery Plan

**What:** Build a concrete plan to recover online scores from 4.6.
**When:** Now — this is urgent.
**How:**
1. Identify the likely root causes (operational standards, specific complaints, specific items)
2. Separate: what affects quality at the source vs. what affects perception at delivery
3. Propose specific, measurable operational changes
4. Define how to track improvement (score, complaint rate)
5. Set a target score and timeline

## Function: Marketing Readiness Assessment

**What:** Determine whether operations are ready for a marketing push.
**When:** Before activating the influencer partnership or any paid marketing.
**How:**
1. Check: are online scores above 4.7? Is the operation clean and consistent?
2. Check: is Henrique's dependency mitigated (secondary cook exists)?
3. Check: can the operation handle a demand spike without quality collapse?
4. If yes to all three: proceed to marketing planning
5. If no: identify the specific blocker and address it first
