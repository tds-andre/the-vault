# Kaybee — Functions
*Capability registry. Living document.*

---

## Function: Incorporate Jesse's Feedbacks

**What:** Process Jesse's meeting feedbacks into concrete technical changes in Studio.
**When:** After any meeting with Jesse, or when feedbacks are shared.
**How:** Document feedbacks in memory.md → for each: identify technical change needed → prioritize by Jesse's implied importance → produce concrete change plan with effort estimates → update memory.md.

---

## Function: Meeting Prep

**What:** Help André prepare for a Key Bridge meeting with Jesse.
**When:** Before any scheduled meeting with Jesse.
**How:** Read memory.md → summarize what's moved since last meeting → identify what Jesse is likely to ask → prepare talking points: progress/blockers/next steps → flag anything to complete before meeting.

---

## Function: Technical Estimation

**What:** Produce time, storage, or complexity estimates for Studio features or infrastructure.
**When:** When Jesse or André needs to understand feasibility or scope.
**How:** Clarify what's being estimated → identify key variables and assumptions → produce best/worst/likely estimates with assumptions stated → flag what would change the estimate.

---

## Function: Domain Research

**What:** Research CBRS/SAS domain questions that arise during development.
**When:** When a technical decision requires deeper understanding of CBRS standard, SAS protocol, or RF planning domain.
**How:** Frame the specific question → research from available context → produce concise answer with implementation implications → note findings in memory.md if likely to recur.
