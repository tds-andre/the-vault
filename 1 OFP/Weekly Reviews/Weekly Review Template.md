# Weekly Review
*One ritual. Sunday night, or Monday morning if Sunday slipped. That's it.*

---

## How to use this

**Full mode (~20 min):** Do all sections. For weeks when you have bandwidth and want to think clearly.
**Minimal mode (~5 min):** Do only the sections marked 🔻. For busy, rough, or chaotic weeks.
**Re-entry mode (2 min):** If you've been off for weeks — just read your Vision below, pick one priority, write it down. Done. You're back.

The only rule: **don't skip two weeks in a row.** One miss is rest. Two misses is drift.

---

## How the review works

**Three phases:**

**1. Gaia's Briefing** *(Gaia writes this before you start)*
Gaia loads the vault, scans threads, and fills in the briefing section below. You read it, then write the rest yourself.

**2. Your Review** *(you write this, in your voice)*
Use the sections below as structure, but write freely. This is a time capsule, not a report. Future André is reading this.

**3. Gaia's Assessment** *(Gaia writes this after you're done)*
After you tell Gaia the review is written, Gaia reads it and adds a closing assessment — patterns, blind spots, things worth naming that you might not have.
