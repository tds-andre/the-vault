## How to use this

**Full mode (~20 min):** Do all sections. For weeks when you have bandwidth and want to think clearly.
**Minimal mode (~5 min):** Do only the sections marked 🔻. For busy, rough, or chaotic weeks.
**Re-entry mode (2 min):** If you've been off for weeks — just read your Vision below, pick one priority, write it down. Done. You're back.

The only rule: **don't skip two weeks in a row.** One miss is rest. Two misses is drift.
