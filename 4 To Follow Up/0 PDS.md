Personal Development System
## Goals
- Provide a **simple** framework for progressing life
- That is **robust** to varying levels of adherence, i.e., it is easy to restart

## Baseline: Journaling
1) **Mandatory**: Write the date, hour and place
2) **Mandatory**: Write one thing that you're grateful for
3) What is on your mind this day (theme of the day/week)
4) Time you slept and time you woke up
5) 3 things you want to accomplish this day
6) Your appointments for the day
